Critical Illness

PRUMy Critical Care

A complete critical illness solution — built around your needs, not a one-size-fits-all plan.

PRUMy Critical Care is a solution, not a single rider: it combines PRUWith You Plus as the base plan with your choice of critical illness riders — Total Multi Crisis Care, Critical Care Plus, Critical Care, Essential Cancer Care and Essential Child Plus. You pick the protection tier that fits your life and budget.

Why this plan

Key Benefits

🧩

Three Protection Tiers

Essential (affordable, specific needs), Enhanced (grows with your responsibilities) or Comprehensive (160 conditions, multiple claims) — mix riders to match your stage of life.

🩺

Expert Care at Every Stage

Case Management Service: second medical opinions from 50,000+ top experts, personalised physician case managers and specialist matching (rider sum assured RM250,000 and above).

🏡

Homecare Programme

Recovery support at home — trained nurses, caregivers, ADL assistance and family caregiving training, with a voucher worth 8% of your rider sum assured (up to RM25,000).

Plan Highlights

  • Choose from 5 CI riders on a PRUWith You Plus base plan
  • Coverage options from cancer-focused up to 160 conditions
  • Assurance Upgrade Privilege: +50% coverage at life milestones without underwriting
  • Case Management and Homecare value-added services (SA RM250k+)
  • Components can be bought separately — no obligation to take the full package
