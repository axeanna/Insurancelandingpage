Legacy Planning

Legacy Settlement Option

Decide exactly how your loved ones receive your legacy.

The Legacy Settlement Option is a free facility on eligible Prudential policies that lets you structure how your death benefit is paid out — as a lump sum, structured instalments over 5–35 years, or milestone-based payouts tied to life events.

Why this plan

Key Benefits

💰

Lump Sum

The classic option: your nominees receive the full benefit immediately, in their chosen proportions.

📆

Structured Instalments

Monthly or yearly instalments over 5 to 35 years — like a continuing salary for your family, protecting them from spending the benefit too quickly.

🎓

Milestone-Based Payouts

Release funds when nominees reach life events you choose — such as university graduation or marriage (up to 3 events per nominee).

Plan Highlights

  • Free to set up on eligible Prudential policies
  • Mix payout styles per nominee — each can be different
  • Instalments from 5 to 35 years, monthly or yearly
  • Leave a customised personal message for your nominees
  • Update the arrangement as your wishes change

Who is this for?

👤 Parents with young children
👤 Anyone concerned about lump-sum mismanagement
👤 Purpose-driven legacy planners
