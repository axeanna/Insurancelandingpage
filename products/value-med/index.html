PRUValue Med

Flexible, affordable medical coverage that works with what you already have.

PRUValue Med covers your hospitalisation and surgical expenses with flexible Med Saver and Deductible options — so you can pair it with an employer plan and keep premiums low, or use it as standalone coverage.

ℹ️ This is a rider that attaches to a base plan such as PRUWith You Plus. Speak to Annabel to find the right combination for your needs.

Key Benefits

Med Saver & Deductible Options

Choose how much you self-cover first — the higher the amount, the lower your premium.

Works With Employer Plans

Full Hospital Coverage
