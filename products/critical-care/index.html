Critical Illness

Critical Care

Essential coverage for 80 late-stage critical illnesses.

Critical Care is an accelerated critical illness rider providing a lump sum payout of 100% of the rider sum assured upon diagnosis of any of 80 covered conditions.

ℹ️ This is a rider that attaches to a base plan such as PRUWith You Plus. Speak to Annabel to find the right combination for your needs.
Why this plan

Key Benefits

🛡️

80 Conditions

Lump-sum protection across 80 late-stage critical illnesses.

💵

100% Lump Sum Payout

Full rider sum assured on diagnosis, plus 10% (max RM25,000) for angioplasty prior to a major claim.

💡

Affordable Entry Point

As an accelerated rider, it's a cost-effective way to add CI protection to your base plan.
