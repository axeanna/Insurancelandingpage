PRUCash Enrich

Guaranteed yearly cash payments for your family's financial future.

PRUCash Enrich is an insurance savings plan with guaranteed annual cash payments from the end of year 1 until maturity, a lump sum maturity benefit with potential upside — and guaranteed acceptance with no medical underwriting.

Why this plan

Key Benefits

💵

Guaranteed Annual Cash

Receive guaranteed cash payments every year from the end of policy year 1 until maturity.

🎯

Maturity Lump Sum

A guaranteed lump sum at maturity, with potential non-guaranteed upside on top.

✅

Guaranteed Acceptance

No medical underwriting on the basic plan — entry age from 14 days to 70 years old.

Plan Highlights

  • Guaranteed yearly Survival Benefit from end of year 1

  • Guaranteed acceptance — no health questions on basic plan
  • Compassionate Benefit payable upon death
  • Optional premium waiver riders for full peace of mind
