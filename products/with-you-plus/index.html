Life & Protection

PRUWith You Plus

A life plan that grows with you — and extends to your loved ones too.

PRUWith You Plus is a life insurance and investment plan in one, with coverage that automatically increases over time and a policy term that extends up to age 100. It even protects your young children at no extra cost.

Why this plan

Key Benefits

📈

Sum Assured Booster

Your sum assured grows 1% every year, up to 50% of the Basic Sum Assured — at no extra cost. Paid upon death, TPD or maturity at age 100.

👶

Child Benefit

All your children are automatically protected from birth to age 7 with death and child-illness coverage at an extra 30% of your Basic Sum Assured — no underwriting, no extra cost.

🎯

Goal Achievement Benefit

A one-time RM500 cash reward when you hit a life milestone: travelling to 10 countries, doubling your salary, owning a home, getting married or welcoming a child.

Plan Highlights

  • Coverage automatically extends up to age 100
  • Sum Assured Booster grows your protection every year from policy year 2
  • Children covered from birth to age 7 at no extra cost
  • RM500 milestone cash reward (entry age 25 and below)
  • Customise with medical, critical illness, accident and payor riders

Who is this for?

👤 Young adults starting their career
👤 Newlyweds and new parents
👤 Anyone who wants protection that grows with life
