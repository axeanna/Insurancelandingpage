Total Multi Crisis Care

Comprehensive coverage for 160 conditions — without touching your life coverage.

Total Multi Crisis Care is an additional critical illness rider covering 160 early-to-late-stage conditions. Claims do not reduce your basic sum assured, and you can claim multiple times up to 400%.

ℹ️ This is a rider that attaches to a base plan such as PRUWith You Plus. Speak to Annabel to find the right combination for your needs.
Why this plan

Key Benefits

160 Conditions

From early to late stage — the widest coverage tier in the critical illness range.
