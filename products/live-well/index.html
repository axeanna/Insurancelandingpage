Life & Protection

PRULive Well

Guaranteed monthly income if disability strikes — and your premiums back at maturity.

PRULive Well pays you a guaranteed monthly income if you become unable to perform basic Activities of Daily Living (ADL), waives your future premiums, and refunds 100% of premiums paid if you reach maturity.

Why this plan

Key Benefits

💵

Monthly Income Benefit

Up to 20 years of guaranteed monthly income upon disability: 50% of your chosen amount if unable to perform 2 ADL, 100% if unable to perform 3 or more.

💰

100% Premium Refund

Choose a policy term to age 80, 90 or 100 — reach maturity and receive a refund of total premiums paid (less any income benefit already received).

🛡️

Legacy Protection

Your loved ones receive a death benefit of 250× the Monthly Income Benefit, and premiums are waived once disability is claimed.

Plan Highlights

  • Guaranteed monthly income for up to 20 years upon ADL disability
  • Premium waiver upon disability keeps coverage running
  • 100% premium refund by maturity (age 80, 90 or 100 options)
  • Death benefit of 250× Monthly Income Benefit
  • Large Sum Assured Discount up to 12% for higher coverage

Who is this for?

👤 Working adults who rely on their income
👤 Those planning for long-term care costs
👤 Anyone without disability income coverage
Good to know

Frequently Asked Questions

ADL are essential self-care tasks: transfer, mobility, continence, dressing, bathing/washing and eating. The benefit is triggered when the inability to perform 2 (50% payout) or 3+ (100% payout) ADL persists for at least 6 months and is permanent.
Retirement or nursing home care can cost RM4,000–RM6,000 per person per month, and RM10,000+ for premium assisted living. A guaranteed income protects your savings and eases the load on family caregivers.
If you hold the policy to maturity, you receive a lump sum refund of all premiums paid (less any Monthly Income Benefit received).
