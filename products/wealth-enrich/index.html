PRUWealth Enrich 2.0

High protection, loyalty rewards, and a legacy that lasts beyond your lifetime.

PRUWealth Enrich 2.0 combines high life coverage with wealth growth and legacy planning — including boosted coverage, accidental death protection of up to 500% extra, and loyalty rewards along the way.

Why this plan

Key Benefits

🚀

Cover Booster

Up to 50% extra coverage on top of your Basic Sum Assured for death or TPD (up to 30% for BSA below RM2 million).

✈️

Up to +500% Accidental Cover

Additional payout of 100%–500% of BSA for accidental death, depending on circumstances — e.g. public transport, overseas travel, commercial flights or natural disasters.

🎁

Loyalty Rewards

Stay protected and receive Loyalty Bonus and Loyalty Booster worth up to 30% of your BSA in total benefits.
