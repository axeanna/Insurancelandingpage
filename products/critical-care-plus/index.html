Critical Illness

Critical Care Plus

80-condition coverage plus ICU, reconstructive and wellness benefits.

Critical Care Plus is an additional rider covering 80 critical illnesses, with extra benefits most plans don't have: ICU Shield, Reconstructive Care, a Wellness Bonus, and upgrade privileges.

ℹ️ This is a rider that attaches to a base plan such as PRUWith You Plus. Speak to Annabel to find the right combination for your needs.
Why this plan

Key Benefits

🏥

80 Conditions + Extras

100% of rider sum assured on diagnosis of 80 covered conditions, plus 10% (max RM25,000) for angioplasty.

🚨

ICU Shield Benefit

10% of sum assured (capped RM250,000) if admitted to ICU for 3 continuous days requiring essential life support.

💪

Reconstructive Care & Wellness

20% of sum assured for covered reconstructive surgery, and a 20% Wellness Bonus (entry age 50 and below).

Plan Highlights

  • Additional rider — claims don't reduce your life coverage
  • 80 late-stage critical illness conditions
  • ICU Shield Benefit: 10% of sum assured, payable once
  • Reconstructive Care Benefit: 20% of sum assured
  • Value-added services (case management, homecare) for SA RM250k+

Who is this for?

👤 Working professionals
👤 Parents
👤 Anyone building core CI protection
