Critical Illness

PRUMan & PRULady

Gender-specific protection built around men's and women's health.

PRUMan and PRULady cover the illnesses most relevant to each gender — from male and female cancers to fertility, pregnancy and mental wellness support — with a unique sum assured bounce-back feature.

Why this plan

Key Benefits

🎗️

Gender-Specific Cancers

100% of sum assured for female cancers (breast, cervix, ovary and more) or male cancers (prostate, testicle and more).

🔄

CIS Bounce-Back

50% payout for Carcinoma-In-Situ — and your full coverage is restored 6 months after diagnosis.

🤰

Beyond Cancer

Recovery and wellness care benefits; PRULady adds fertility care, pregnancy care, baby care and mental wellness support.

Plan Highlights

  • Death & TPD: higher of 100% sum assured or total premiums paid
  • Full payout for gender-specific cancers
  • Carcinoma-In-Situ: 50% payout with sum assured restored after 6 months
  • PRULady: fertility, pregnancy and baby care benefits
  • Wellness care benefits including dermatology and screening support

Who is this for?

👤 Women planning a family
👤 Men who want targeted health protection
👤 Anyone complementing broader CI coverage
