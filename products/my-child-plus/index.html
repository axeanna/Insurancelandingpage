Child & Family

PRUMy Child Plus

Protection for mummy and baby, from 13 weeks of pregnancy.

PRUMy Child Plus protects both mother and child from as early as 13 weeks of pregnancy — through delivery, childhood conditions, developmental support, and all the way to age 100.

Why this plan

Key Benefits

🤰

Mother Care Benefits

Coverage for pregnancy complications, emergency C-section, maternity cancer, infectious disease and mental wellness support in the first year.

👶

Baby Care Benefits

Hospitalisation cover for 27 congenital conditions and ALL structural congenital conditions (up to RM50,000/year to age 7), neonatal jaundice, ICU/HDU daily benefit and incubation.

🧠

Developmental Support

Yearly coverage up to RM8,000 for child developmental disorders including autism and ADHD treatment and supportive therapy, up to age 7.

Plan Highlights

  • Coverage starts from 13 weeks of pregnancy
  • Congenital conditions covered up to RM50,000 annually to age 7
  • Autism, ADHD and developmental support up to RM8,000/year
  • Child protected to age 100 with Sum Assured Booster (+1%/year up to 50%)
  • Payor Benefit: premiums covered if the unexpected happens to you
  • Easy enrolment for your next pregnancy without full underwriting

Who is this for?

👤 Expecting parents (from 13 weeks)
👤 New parents
👤 Parents planning their next child
